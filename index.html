About Me

I'm Kate, a graphic designer and aspiring minimalist. I thoroughly enjoy simplicity and I believe in things that stand the test of time, not those that merely subscribe to the latest trend. I prioritize functionality and let the aesthetics follow suit. We live in an ever-changing world where efficiency is of utmost importance; I strive to make my designs both beautiful and effective, whether they be for print, web, or anything else.
